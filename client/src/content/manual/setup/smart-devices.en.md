# Connect Smart Devices

This guide explains how to connect TP-Link Tapo smart plugs to BaluHost for energy monitoring and remote control.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to BaluHost
- TP-Link Tapo smart plug on the same network as BaluHost
- Tapo account credentials (email and password)

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Smart Devices** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Device**
3. Select the device type (e.g. "Tapo P110")
4. Enter the IP address of the smart plug
5. Enter your Tapo account email and password
6. Click **Connect** — BaluHost tests the connection automatically
7. The device appears in the overview with real-time energy consumption and on/off control
